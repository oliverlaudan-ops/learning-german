Grammar Topics

A2 Articles

Articles: der, die, das

Learn the three grammatical genders and when to use each article.

Quick Reference

der - masculine (der Mann, der Tisch)

die - feminine (die Frau, die Lampe)

das - neuter (das Kind, das Buch)

A2 Cases

Nominativ & Akkusativ

Master the subject and direct object cases. Only masculine changes!

Quick Reference

Nominativ: der Mann (subject)

Akkusativ: den Mann (direct object)

Feminine, neuter, and plural don't change.
